RevShare Explained for iGaming Affiliates

Revenue share (RevShare) pays you a percentage of the net revenue generated by the players you refer, for as long as they keep playing. It is the foundation of long-term affiliate income. DBBET Partners offers up to 55% RevShare through its partner program.

How RevShare is calculated

Net revenue is typically a player's losses minus bonuses, fees and chargebacks. Your RevShare percentage is applied to that figure, paid out monthly for the lifetime of the account.

A 55% RevShare on a player who nets the operator $200 in a month earns you $110 that month, and similar amounts in future months if the player stays active.

Why lifetime value is everything

Unlike CPA, RevShare rewards player quality over raw volume. One loyal high-value player can out-earn dozens of one-time depositors over a year.

This makes content quality and audience trust crucial. Players who arrive via genuine recommendations tend to stay longer and play more responsibly, boosting your sustainable income.

Maximising your RevShare income

Focus on GEOs and verticals with strong retention. Promote responsibly so players stick around, and keep your top placements for the brands with the best terms and product.

Negotiate higher tiers as your volume grows. Programs like DBBET Partners scale RevShare up to 55%, so consistent quality traffic can unlock the top rate.

Key takeaways

  • RevShare pays a percentage of referred players' net revenue, monthly
  • It rewards player quality and retention over one-time volume
  • Top tiers reach 55%; quality traffic unlocks the best rates
