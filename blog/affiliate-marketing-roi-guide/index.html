Measuring ROI the right way

True ROI compares all costs, including time, content and paid traffic, against net commissions actually received, not headline rates.

Track net income per click by source and GEO so you can see exactly which efforts pay and which drain your budget.

Improving ROI across channels

Cut or fix underperforming sources, double down on winners, and test new creatives systematically rather than randomly.

RevShare improves ROI over time as referred players keep paying, so factor lifetime value into your calculations, not just first deposits.

Scaling profitably

Only scale channels with proven positive ROI, and reinvest profits gradually so you can react if performance shifts.
