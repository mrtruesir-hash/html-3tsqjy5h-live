Why headline rates can mislead

A 60% RevShare on a weak product with poor retention can earn less than a 50% deal on a brand players love. The real driver is revenue per player times your share.

Always look past the percentage to conversion, retention and payment reliability. A high rate you never actually receive is worthless.

How to compare true earnings

Run the same traffic to two programs and compare net income per click over a few weeks. This reveals the genuinely higher-paying option for your audience.

Factor in payout thresholds and methods. A program that pays reliably and on time beats one with a higher rate and constant delays.
