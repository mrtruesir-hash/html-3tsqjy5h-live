Best Affiliate Programs for Africa 2026: Egypt, Nigeria, Kenya

Africa is one of the fastest-growing iGaming markets in the world, driven by young, mobile-first audiences and rapid smartphone adoption. For affiliates, that means high-intent traffic and far less competition than saturated Western markets. This guide covers the regulatory picture, the payment methods that actually convert, and how to pick a program for African traffic.

The regulatory picture

Regulation across Africa is fragmented but increasingly clear. Nigeria and Kenya have established licensing frameworks and active, large betting audiences. Egypt is more restrictive, so affiliates should promote responsibly and target only where a brand is licensed. Always check that the program you join holds the right licences for the country you are sending traffic from, and keep 18+ and responsible-gambling messaging visible.

Payment methods that convert

Mobile money is king in Africa. Programs and brands that support local rails — M-Pesa in Kenya, mobile wallets and bank transfers in Nigeria, and local options in Egypt — convert far better than card-only flows. When you compare programs, weight payment localisation heavily; it directly affects deposit rates and therefore your RevShare.

What to look for in an Africa-focused program

  • Local licensing in your target countries.
  • Mobile-money payments for players, and reliable affiliate payouts.
  • Localised landing pages and lightweight pages that load on slow connections.
  • Responsive support that understands African markets.
