Commission and earning potential

Both programs offer RevShare and CPA. DBBET Partners advertises up to 55% RevShare plus a hybrid option and a 10% sub-affiliate tier, which gives you more ways to structure earnings as you scale. Parimatch is an established brand with competitive tiered rates that reward higher volumes. If you want flexible models early on, the broader DBBET structure is worth a look — see the full breakdown on the partner benefits page.

Approval speed and support

For new affiliates, fast approval and a responsive manager matter more than a fraction of a percent in RevShare. DBBET Partners emphasises 24/7 support and quick onboarding. Parimatch has a large, experienced team but onboarding times can vary by region.

GEO coverage

This is often the deciding factor. DBBET focuses heavily on emerging markets across Africa, Asia and MENA, with local payment support that converts well in those regions. Parimatch has strong recognition in CIS and parts of Asia. Match the program to where your audience actually is — if you run African or South Asian traffic, see our Africa affiliate guide.

Verdict

If you are an established affiliate with high CIS volume, Parimatch is a solid choice. If you want flexible commission models, fast approval, 24/7 support and strong emerging-market coverage, DBBET Partners is the better starting point for most new and growth-stage affiliates.
