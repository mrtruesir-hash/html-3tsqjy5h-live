Affiliate Fraud Prevention for iGaming Affiliates

Fraud is the fastest way to lose an affiliate account and clawed-back earnings. Understanding and preventing it protects your business. Programs value clean traffic, as reflected in the DBBET partner benefits.

What counts as affiliate fraud

Fraud includes incentivised sign-ups, bot traffic, self-referrals, fake accounts and misleading promotions. All of it harms operators and gets affiliates banned.

Even unintentional issues, like buying low-quality traffic, can look like fraud to detection systems and trigger reviews.

Why clean traffic pays better

Operators reward affiliates who send genuine, converting players with better terms and faster payouts.

Clean traffic also protects your lifetime RevShare, since real players keep playing while fake ones get removed and reversed.

Practical fraud prevention

Vet your traffic sources, avoid incentivised methods, monitor your analytics for anomalies, and follow each program's terms closely.

Lean on 24/7 support to flag and resolve suspicious patterns early, before they put your account at risk.

Key takeaways

  • Fraud includes incentivised, bot, self-referral and fake-account traffic
  • Clean traffic earns better terms, faster payouts and protected RevShare
  • Vet sources, monitor analytics and follow program terms to stay safe
