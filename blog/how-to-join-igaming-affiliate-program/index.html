How to Join an iGaming Affiliate Program: Step-by-Step Guide

Joining an iGaming affiliate program is one of the fastest ways to turn gambling-related traffic into recurring revenue. This guide walks you through eligibility, the application process, approval timelines and the first steps to take once you are accepted. If you already know you are ready, you can jump straight to the DBBET Partners programme.

Quick summary

An iGaming affiliate program pays you to send players to a sportsbook or casino. You apply, get approved (often within 24-48 hours), grab your tracking links, and start earning RevShare, CPA or a hybrid of both. The key to fast approval is a clear traffic source and an honest application.

Step 1: Check the eligibility requirements

Before you apply, make sure you meet the basic criteria. Most reputable iGaming affiliate programs expect you to be 18 or older, to operate a legitimate traffic source (a website, social channel, Telegram community, YouTube channel or paid-media account), and to comply with responsible-gambling rules in the markets you target. You do not need huge volume to start, but you do need a real, describable way to reach players.

Programs also check that your traffic is allowed in the regions where the brand holds a licence. If your audience is mainly in Africa, Asia or the Middle East, look for a partner with strong coverage in those GEOs.

Step 2: Complete the application

The application itself is short. You will typically provide your contact details, your main traffic source and its size, the GEOs you cover, and your preferred commission model. Be specific and honest: managers approve applications faster when they can clearly see where your players will come from. Our step-by-step instructions explain exactly what to enter.

Step 3: Wait for approval

The better programs review applications within 24 to 48 hours. A responsive affiliate manager is a good sign you have chosen a serious partner. While you wait, prepare your landing pages and decide which offers to promote first.

Step 4: Get your tracking links and creatives

Once approved, you gain access to a dashboard with unique tracking links, banners, landing pages and real-time statistics. Place links naturally in your content, label gambling promotions responsibly, and always include 18+ messaging.

Step 5: Your first steps as a new affiliate

Start small and measure. Add one or two offers to your best content, watch how clicks convert to registrations and deposits, and ask your manager which GEOs and offers perform best. Reinvest in what works.

Key takeaways

  • You can usually apply and get approved within 24-48 hours with a clear traffic source.
  • Honesty on the application is the single biggest factor in fast approval.
  • Choose a partner whose licences cover your audience GEOs.
  • Start with one offer, measure, then scale what converts.
