AFFILIATE GUIDE

How to Succeed with DBBET

A complete step-by-step guide to the DBBET affiliate program, commission models and tracking tools.

01

How the Affiliate Program Works

Sign up, get approved and receive your unique tracking links and promo codes. Share them with your audience, and every player who registers and deposits through your link is permanently attributed to your account. You earn commission on their activity according to your chosen deal.

02

How Promo Codes Work

Promo codes are short, memorable codes players enter at registration. They are ideal for video, audio and offline channels where clickable links are not practical. Any player who signs up with your code is tracked to your account just like a referral link.

03

How Referral Links Work

Your referral link contains a unique tracking ID. When a user clicks it, a cookie records the visit and ties any subsequent registration and deposits to you. Links can point to the homepage or specific landing pages optimized for sports, casino or slots.

04

How Revenue Share Commission Works

You earn a percentage (up to 55%) of the net gaming revenue generated by your referred players for their lifetime. Net revenue is player losses minus winnings, bonuses and processing fees. The more active your players, the higher your recurring income.

05

How CPA Commission Works

Cost Per Acquisition pays a fixed amount (up to $110) for each referred player who completes the qualifying action — usually registering and making a first deposit above the minimum. CPA gives predictable, upfront income per converted player.

06

How the Hybrid Model Works

The hybrid deal combines CPA and Revenue Share: you receive a fixed payout when a player first deposits, plus an ongoing share of their net revenue. It balances immediate cash flow with long-term passive income.

07

How Sub-Affiliate Links Work

Invite other affiliates using your sub-affiliate link. When they join under you and start earning, you receive an additional commission (up to 10%) of their earnings — at no cost to them. It is a powerful way to scale your income through a network.

08

Statistics & Dashboard Guide

Your affiliate dashboard provides real-time reporting and tracking. Monitor clicks, unique visitors, registrations, first-time deposits, active players and earnings in one place. Filter by date, campaign, country and traffic source, and export detailed analytics to optimize your campaigns and maximize conversions.
